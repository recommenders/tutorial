NordiCHI 2016: Subjective and Objective Replication and Reproducibility

Tutorial at the 9th Nordich Conference on Human-Computer Interaction. Focus on validation of data and methods from a replication and reproducibility perspective both qualitatively collected data (user studies, surveys, video recordings) and quantitative data collected through information access systems (movie recommendation, playlist curation, etc).

RecSys 2015: Replicable Evaluation of Recommender Systems

Tutorial at the 8th ACM Conference on Recommender Systems. Focus on replicable evaluation of recommender systems highlighting differences between replicability and reproducibility. The tutorial presents methods for reporting on experiments (data management, design choices, algorithms, software) in order to alleviate unbiased comparison across experimental settings.
